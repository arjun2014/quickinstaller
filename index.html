The Modern Way to Set Up Your PC

Stop wasting hours manually downloading installers. QuickInstaller is a fast software installer that streamlines your entire setup process into a single, automated script.

Time-Saving Automation

Why spend hours clicking through installers? As a bulk app installer, QuickInstaller lets you install multiple apps at once, turning a tedious chore into a simple, one-click process.

Safe & Clean Installs

Our silent installer for apps leverages the trusted Chocolatey repository. This means no bloatware or hidden adware—just the clean, official versions of the software you want.

Always Up-to-Date

Forget searching for the latest versions. QuickInstaller automatically fetches the most recent stable release of every application, ensuring your new PC setup starts with modern, secure software.
